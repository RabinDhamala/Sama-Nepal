Sama Nepal is a non-profit organization established in 2015, registered in government of Nepal and affiliated with the Social Welfare Coucil of Nepal.


We promote barrier free, inclusive and right-based opportunities to everyone excluded from right to education.


Right now, we are working in four districts of the country (Banke, Bhaktapur, Lalitpur and Sindhupalchowk). Our Inclusion Facilitators together with other associated experts from various disciplines are working in the field and are supported by our administrative team in our central office in Bhaktapur.

Our Objectives

Sama Nepal's objectives are:

  • to create an inclusive situationfor everyone who is in need (able and differently able).
  • to promote barrier free, inclusive and right-based opportunities to everyone excluded from right to education.
  • to move from segregation to inclusion by bringing everyone into mainstream education.
  • to establish an inclusion model which can be replicated.
