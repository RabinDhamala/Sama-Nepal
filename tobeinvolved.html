Let's join Sama Nepal
for Inclusion!



There are several ways to get involved with Sama Nepal

according to your possibilities and your location.



Become a volunteer of Sama Nepal



If you want to live a unique experience, you can join us as a volunteer or intern! Thank you for contacting us to build together a porject according to your skills, your availability and our needs.

Become an online volunteer of Sama Nepal


If you want to help us from your home, this is also possible! You can help us design new assistive devices, make research for us, create and translate documents,...

Support Us



If you are interested in our work, you can also help by providing us a financial support. Tax-deductible donations are available from Europe. You can also organize your own fundraising event.

Whichever way you want to get involved with Sama Nepal, thank you for contacting us.
